About Us

My name is Oscar. I am a local tour agent who has been working in the tourism industry in Cozumel for over 20 years. I have had the privilege of guiding thousands of tourists from all over the world to enjoy this natural paradise in a safe, affordable and exclusive way.

My experience has taught me that giving my best to each person is my best presentation. I grew up on the island and that's why I only work with the best without taking any risks and offering a personalized service to guarantee a great experience. Now I lead my own tourist agency and I have a team that will be at your service.
